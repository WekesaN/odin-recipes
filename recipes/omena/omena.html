How to prepare Omena!

Description

Boil the omena, dry it on the sun for some hours, deep fry it then add the other ingredients

Ingredients Used

  • Two onions
  • Three tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Turmeric
  • One royco cube
  • cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cut the onions and cooking oil on the sufuria, turn them until they turn golden brown
  2. Add the tomatoes, Turmeric, royco cube, tomato paste
  3. Add the omena with some water
  4. serve with ugali when ready
