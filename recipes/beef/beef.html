How to prepare Beef

Description

Boil the meat until its tender before cooking it

Ingredients Used

  • Two onions
  • Three tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Turmeric
  • One royco cube
  • cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cut the onions and cooking oil on the sufuria, turn them until they turn golden brown
  2. Add the tomatoes, Turmeric, royco cube, tomato paste
  3. Add the meat with some water
  4. serve with ugali when ready
